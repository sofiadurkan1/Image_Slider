Anywhere We Roam!

Once a year, go someplace you’ve never been before.It can be a new country, a new city, or an interesting spot near your hometown.

It is better to travel well than to arrive.

Traveling – it leaves you speechless, then turns you into a storyteller. The journey changes you; it should change you. It leaves marks on your memory, on your consciousness, on your heart, and on your body. You take something with you. Hopefully, you leave something good behind.”

Oh, the places you’ll go.

Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you didn’t do than by the ones you did do. So throw off the bowlines. Catch the trade winds in your sails. Explore!

Life is short and the world is wide.

To awaken quite alone in a strange town is one of the most pleasant sensations in the world
